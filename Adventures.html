Yercaud

Premium tent stay or cottages and the best of trail riding locations. We’ve got you covered. Yercaud is a charming hill station located in the Shevaroys range of the Western Ghats in Tamil Nadu, India. Perched at an altitude of about 4,970 feet above sea level, it offers a cooler climate and picturesque landscapes. With some brilliant dense trails starting from Level 1 to Level 4, this place has everything to offer for an adventurer.

Coorg

Coorg, also known as “The Scotland of India” is a picturesque district in the Western Ghats of Karnataka, Renowned for its lush landscapes, pleasant climate, and rich cultural heritage. The terrain in this area becomes completely different during climate changes and makes the off-road trails either extremely slushy or dusty. The way we like it. And the icing on the cake here is riding through the forest, beautiful views around, and plenty of river/stream crossings.
