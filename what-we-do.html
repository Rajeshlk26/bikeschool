What We Do

Mud Master Adventures is dedicated to providing premium, sustainable off-road motorcycle experiences.

01 Focus on Premium, Sustainable Off-road Experiences

Unlike many competitors who offer a broad range of adventure activities, Mud Master Adventures is dedicated exclusively to premium off-road motorcycle experiences. This specialization allows us to provide in-depth, high-quality experiences with a focus on safety, skill enhancement, and exploration of unique trails.

02 Small Group Sizes for Personalized Attention

We limit our group sizes to 8-10 riders to ensure personalized attention and a more intimate, cohesive adventure experience. This contrasts with some competitors who cater to larger groups, potentially compromising the personalized nature of the trips.

03 Expert Leadership and Rider Training

Our team comprises experienced riders with extensive knowledge of off-road trails and advanced riding techniques. We provide comprehensive training and support, which is a significant differentiator from competitors who may not offer such focused skill enhancement programs.

04 Exploration of Unique and Private Trails

Mud Master Adventures emphasizes exploring less-traveled, exclusive trails through forests, plantations, and wildlife areas. This offers a more adventurous and unique experience compared to the more common and well-trodden paths offered by other tour operators.

05 Strong Focus on Safety and Trust

Safety is our top priority. Our tours include detailed pre-trip briefings, safety demonstrations, and on-trail support. We also ensure that our equipment and gear are of the highest standards, providing peace of mind to our clients.

FAQs

Is there a specific bike I need to own to join these rides?

Yes, two wheels and an engine 200cc and above.

I am new to this genre of riding and I have no clue about the riding gears needed.

You have come to the right place. We can suggest a budget to top of the line of the riding gears and direct you to places that retail them.

I am a highway tourer, can I use the same gears for such rides?

As long as you are comfortable in them, you are good to go. But after a few sessions, you will realize the difference between touring gear and adventure touring gear.

I keep dropping or crashing the motorcycle, what happens here?

Small spills and falls are part and parcel of such rides. Be prepared to have scratches and battle scars on your motorcycle and riding gear. Get up, click a photo, laugh it off and carry on. But if something goes downhill, we are first aid trained and have local hospitals in the areas we travel. All medical bills and supplies will have to be taken care of by the participant or respective insurance.

What if my bike breaks down?

They are machines after all. Basic assistance will be given. We will not carry spare parts for any of the motorcycles. On such rides, it's always better to carry a gear shifter, rear brake pedal and a clutch lever. Off course a small and compact tool kit as well.

I don’t wish to do such rides in the monsoons.

You can opt for any of the other rides in the calendar, but hey! We cannot assure you about the weather conditions. You are here to get dirty and sliding, the monsoons just make it even better.

I would like to bring a pillion along. Is that allowed?

We do not recommend having a pillion rider on our trails, however they are free to join and spend time around the property we are staying while we are on the trails. By the way, the properties we have shortlisted for all our rides are located in a super scenic spot and rest assured you will love it.

How does a cancellation work just in case?

We book our stays way in advance and have limited slots for people to sign up. We understand that emergency situations can arise at any given time. If the cancellation request is put across 6 days before the ride, we will charge 30% cancellation fees, anything later than that, we will not be able to refund the booking amount.

Does your booking amount include sharing or non sharing rooms?

The prices shared with you at the time of booking are on a sharing basis, but if you’d like to have your own space, we can help you out with a slightly different pricing.

Alright, you guys seem to be awesome, the pictures and videos on your website look amazing. But why should we choose you when there are other companies too?

Our adventures are very different when compared to other companies and most of our trail riding happens in a private property, lovely scenic locations, premium stay, good quality food and make sure you have a good time. All this is inclusive of what you pay, excluding the booze. That said and done, you are about to go on an adventure of a lifetime and make memories.
